Application Guide

Simple to Apply.
Perfect Every Time.

Follow these five steps for a professional, crack-free finish on every job.

01
🧱
Surface Preparation

Clean the wall thoroughly — remove dust, oil, loose plaster, and any contaminants. Ensure the substrate is sound and structurally firm.

💡 Lightly dampen the surface before applying — this improves adhesion and prevents the wall from pulling moisture out of the skim too fast.
02
🪣
Mixing

Pour 6–7 litres of clean, cool water into a mixing drum. Gradually add Parmafix powder while stirring. Mix for 2–3 minutes until completely smooth and lump-free.

💡 Always add powder to water — never the other way around.
03
🔧
Application

Apply with a clean steel trowel in a 2–4 mm layer. Work from top to bottom in even, sweeping motions. Apply a second coat if needed after partial setting.

💡 Don't overwork the surface while wet — apply evenly and leave it to set before finishing.
04
⏱️
Setting & Drying

Allow 20–30 minutes initial set before polishing. Full drying takes 24–48 hours depending on temperature, humidity, and coat thickness.

💡 Avoid direct sun or strong wind during drying — it can cause surface cracks. Cover openings if necessary.
05
✅
Final Finish

Once partially set, polish the surface using a damp sponge trowel in circular motions. For an ultra-smooth finish, apply a final light pass with a clean steel trowel.

💡 Light sanding with 120-grit before painting gives a perfect final surface. Paint only after full drying (24–48 hrs).

FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything fundis, contractors, and hardware shops ask about Parmafix.

Does Parmafix crack after drying?
+
No. Parmafix contains a polymer blend specifically formulated to resist hairline cracking — even in Kenya's heat and humidity changes. Provided the surface is properly prepared and the correct water ratio is used, cracking is not a concern.
How much water do I add per bag?
+
Add 6–7 litres of clean, cool water per 25 kg bag. Always add the powder gradually to the water and mix for 2–3 minutes until smooth and lump-free.
What is the coverage per bag?
+
One 25 kg bag covers 18–22 m² at a 2–4 mm application thickness. Coverage varies slightly depending on surface texture and application technique.
How long before I can paint over Parmafix?
+
Allow a minimum of 24–48 hours for the skim to fully dry before painting. Always wait for a fully white, dry surface.
What surfaces can Parmafix be applied on?
+
Parmafix works on concrete walls, sand cement plaster, blocks, and brick. The surface must be clean, dry, and structurally sound.
